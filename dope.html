Dope

What is Dope

Our App solves the problem of Rap music production tools, for those who are engaged in this discipline and have the need to produce their own music.

DOPE is a Rap music making application, a simple, easy-to-access application for those who participate in this subject and have the need to produce their own music.

A simple application but still meets all the basic features of a required rap application and makes it easy for users to use to create a demo to save their ideas.‚Äù

My Role

UI & UX DesignPrototypeResearch

Problem definition

Demand for tools to create music for Rap players is higher than ever. However, not all popular rap players can familiarize themselves with and use music production software on computers, complicated manipulations and demanding professional knowledge of music make them feel depressed.

Ordinary rap players also have a common problem that they always have a lot of ideas at uncertain times and places, but cannot save exactly that idea to develop it.

Target User

The audience that our application targets is the majority of young people today aged 18-25 and those who have a passion for rap and directly participate in this subject.

User Research

As people who are directly involved in the rap industry and have been in the rap community for about 2 years, here's what we've observed from rappers in the community's rap-making habits.

The phrase "Hustle makes music" is not too strange when most people who pursue this subject have to work hard to have funds to serve their passion: adding music-making equipment or having money to pay. for studio support services.

With rap songs collaborating with other rappers, they often send each other beats and then come up with ideas. Each rapper with different conditions will record their own part and then put it together into a complete song at an external Studio or their own Studio.

Product ideas are at their best when they sit together and rap freestyle on an existing beat.

In groups that share rap products on facebook, a part of amateur rappers has a need to learn about how to produce rap music. However, tutorials or classes require a fee, leading most amateur rappers to not have the opportunity to learn, so they return to their usual way of making music: composing and paying to complete it. Product improvement at the external Studio.

Most famous rappers or rapper groups are capable of producing their own music. They perceive the color of their music not only from the lyrics, flow or delivery but also from the color of the beat and how they animate their rap on that beat.

New rappers are always looking for a community to exchange and learn from. They see community as an integral part of rap culture.

Interview

After interviewing 5 people, some are rappers and some just love rap music, we got the following results

- 4 out of 5 people have trouble finding a tool to save their rap ideas.

- They have difficulty getting used to and using existing music making software.

- They often come up with ideas and compose music according to their inspiration.

- They want to be free to choose their own beat without being constrained by quantity or price.
